Meridian AI-Powered Regulatory Compliance

Unlocking UK Housing Delivery Through Intelligent Gateway Review

The UK needs 1.5 million new homes. The Building Safety Act 2022 created rigorous Gateway processes-but regulatory bottlenecks now delay even well-designed projects. Meridian is a proprietary AI platform that assesses Gateway 2 submissions before you submit, catching documentation issues early.

Purpose-Built, Not a Wrapper

Meridian is not "upload your documents to ChatGPT and ask for feedback." We've built a proprietary data model specifically for UK building safety regulation-55 deterministic rules derived from Building Regulations, Approved Documents, and BSR guidance, each pre-linked to specific regulatory citations. This is AI customised for this use case, not generic chat.

Full Transparency

Every finding cites the specific regulation (document, section, page) and the exact extract from your submission. Complete auditability-no black box.

Agentic AI with Human-in-the-Loop

Meridian intelligently triages what it can handle vs. what requires human judgement. With your approval, the AI can execute certain changes (formatting, cross-references, structure) while flagging substantive compliance issues for professional review.
