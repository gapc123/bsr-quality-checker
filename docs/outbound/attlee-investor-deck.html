The Real Bottleneck

Post-Grenfell regulation is necessary. The process is broken.

The new regulatory landscape:

  • Building Safety Act 2022
  • Building Safety Regulator (BSR) created
  • Mandatory gateway between planning approval and construction start

Reality today:

  • Only ~1/3 of submissions approved
  • Average review time: ~8 months
  • Capital already committed when delays hit
  • Financing costs inflate projects 15%+
4

The Insight

Most Gateway 2 failures are not unsafe buildings. They are unsafe submissions.

  • Missing documents
  • Incomplete cross-references
  • Inconsistent evidence across reports
  • Errors found too late to fix cheaply

There is no pre-submission intelligence layer.

Developers submit and hope. Rejections cost months. There is no systematic way to check readiness before engaging the regulator.
