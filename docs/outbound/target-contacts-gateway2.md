## Outreach Approach

### Message Angle
- Lead with the problem: "76% of Gateway 2 decisions are London projects, with some waiting 48 weeks"
- Position Meridian as reducing rejection risk: "Only 1/3 of submissions approved first time"
- Differentiate from generic AI: "55 deterministic rules, not ChatGPT"

### Suggested Channels
1. **LinkedIn InMail** - Direct to named contacts
2. **Email** - Company domains (firstname.lastname@company.com pattern)
3. **Warm intro** - Ask for referrals from existing network

### Qualifying Questions
- "How many Gateway 2 submissions do you have in progress?"
- "What's your current first-time approval rate?"
- "How are you managing the documentation quality checks?"
