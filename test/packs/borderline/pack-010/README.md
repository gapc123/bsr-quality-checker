# Test Pack 010 - "Queens Walk" (BORDERLINE QUALITY)

**Type**: Borderline (passed with advisory notes)
**Project**: Queens Walk - residential tower
**Height**: 23m (8 storeys)
**Location**: London (Hackney)
**Gateway 2 Outcome**: Passed with advisory notes - outdated standards

## Project Details

- **Building Use**: Residential (C3) - 98 apartments
- **Height**: 23m (8 storeys above ground, 1 basement level)
- **Construction**: Concrete frame with brick cladding
- **Fire Strategy**: Present (32 pages) but references some outdated standards

## Submission Quality - Borderline

⚠️ Fire strategy references BS 9999:2008 (superseded by BS 9991:2015)
⚠️ Some documents reference 2010 Building Regs (pre-2022 amendments)
⚠️ Sprinkler design references old BS EN 12845:2004 (should be 2015 version)
✅ Core safety requirements met (non-combustible cladding, sprinklers, 2 staircases)
⚠️ London Fire Statement present but brief (8 pages - adequate but not comprehensive)
⚠️ Some document dates inconsistent (fire strategy 2025, structural calcs 2024)
⚠️ Cross-referencing between documents weak

## What Makes This "Borderline"

- **Outdated standards**: Multiple references to superseded standards/regulations
- **Document vintage inconsistency**: Mix of 2024 and 2025 dated documents
- **Adequate but not current**: Meets requirements but doesn't use latest guidance
- **Weak integration**: Documents don't cross-reference well
- **London requirements met minimally**: London Fire Statement present but brief

## Specific Issues

1. **Fire Strategy**: References BS 9999:2008 instead of BS 9991:2015
2. **Sprinklers**: Design calculations cite BS EN 12845:2004 (should be 2015)
3. **Building Regs**: Some sections reference 2010 regs not 2022 amendments
4. **Structural calcs**: Dated 2024 but fire strategy dated 2025 (version control issue)
5. **London Fire Statement**: Only 8 pages (adequate but brief)
6. **PEEP**: Mentioned but not detailed

## Why It Passed

Despite issues, core safety requirements met:
- Non-combustible external walls
- Sprinklers throughout (even if design uses old standard)
- 2 staircases for 23m building
- Fire resistance periods stated (60-min)
- Competent dutyholders appointed
- All required documents present (even if some outdated)

BSR accepted it with **advisory notes** to update standards references before construction.
