Policy D12A
Planning applications for development which
include:
• a new residential or commercial unit, or
• a new lift, or
• alterations to the materials on the
external walls, or
• alterations to the internal or external
communal areas that support the
evacuation strategy for the property
should submit a Planning Fire Safety Strategy
(PFSS).
Where the applicant considers parts of or the
whole policy are not relevant to a
development, this should be justified in a
Reasonable Exception Statement (RES).
See section 4.2 below. This includes all
householder applications.
Policy D12B
Major developments should submit a Fire
Statement.
Section 96a or Section 73 applications to
major developments where elements
addressed in the Fire Statement or secured
by condition such as the access to the site or
building, evacuation strategy or materials are
amended applicants should submit an
(updated) Fire Statement that considers the
amended development as a whole.
Outline planning applications should
submit an Outline Fire Statement. See
section 5.4 below.
