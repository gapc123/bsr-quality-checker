Regulation 3(2)(o)
Higher-risk building occupation phase, the review of a safety case report prepared by the PAP
under section 85 of the act.
Description
Review of the safety case report as may from time to time be required by BSR (for example, not
necessarily connected with either the building assessment certificate process at schedule 15, or
with the PAP notifying BSR that they have updated the safety case report).
(As provided for under section 85 (and any regulations made under section 85(3)) of the act).
Trigger BSR calls in the safety case report for review.
Amount payable £151 per hour worked for BSR staff.
