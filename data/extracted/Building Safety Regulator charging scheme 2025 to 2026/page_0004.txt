To note that the existing exemption for charging for building work solely required for disabled
persons under The Building (Local Authority Charges) Regulations 2010 is being retained and
the regulations have extended this exemption to BSR. The provision exempts building work
solely required to facilitate or improve the buildings use by disabled persons from local authority
building control charges.
