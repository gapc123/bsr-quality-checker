The inspection of buildings including any testing or taking of samples, whether in the design and
construction or occupation phases.
Description
Inspections of buildings including any testing or taking of samples where BSR needs to do so
following completion of building work, this may include any tests or sampling the regulator has
required to be undertaken.
(As provided for under section 33(4) of the Building Act 1984 and under regulation 46 of the
regulations).
Trigger Any complaint, relevant intelligence, incident, or submission of a building
assessment or regularisation certificate application or safety case.
Amount £151 per hour worked for BSR staff.
