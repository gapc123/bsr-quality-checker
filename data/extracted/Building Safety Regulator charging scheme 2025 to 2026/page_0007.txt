Schedule 2
Regulation 3(2)(b)
The determination of an application under section 8(3A) of the Building Act 1984.
Description
Assessment of an application for a direction and decision to dispense with or relax a
requirement where the regulator is the building control authority.
(As provided for in section 8 of the Building Act 1984).
Trigger Submission to the regulator of an application for a direction under section 8(3A)
of the Building Act 1984.
Amount £113 charge with application.
payable
