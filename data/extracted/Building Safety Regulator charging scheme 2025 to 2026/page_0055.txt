Schedule 28
Regulation 3(2)(w)
Any action taken in order to respond to an appeal against a decision of the regulator.
Description
Work done in preparation for and during the course of any appeal against a decision of the
regulator, including any engagement with the appellant to resolve the dispute.
(As provided for under sections 56 and 103 to 106 of the act and schedule 6 of the act).
Trigger Submission of an appeal against a BSR decision.
