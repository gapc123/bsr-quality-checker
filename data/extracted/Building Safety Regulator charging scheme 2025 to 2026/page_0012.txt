Description
Action taken by the regulator in connection with the giving of a notice or grant of a certificate
under section 25(3) of the Building Act 1984.
(As provided for in section 25 of the Building Act 1984).
Trigger Determination by the regulator that a proposal approved in a building control
approval application for providing a supply of water has not been carried into effect
or has not resulted in an appropriate water supply for the occupants of the house.
Amount £151 per hour worked for BSR staff.
payable
Any costs to BSR of relevant authorities and third parties.
