Description
Action which may be taken by BSR under subsections (2), (3) or (5)(a) of section 19 of the
Building Act 1984.
(As provided for in section 19 of the Building Act 1984).
Trigger Where BSR is or becomes aware of the use of materials to which section 19 of the
Building Act 1984 applies and determines action is appropriate under subsections
(2), (3) or (5)(a).
Amount £151 per hour worked for BSR staff.
payable
Any costs to BSR of relevant authorities and third parties.
