Investigation, inspection and testing (including sampling) activities carried out by BSR in
response to an unplanned event.
Description
Investigation, inspection, testing, and other activities relating to a building or dutyholder, and
management arrangements in response to an unplanned event outside the normal BSR
inspection routine.
(As provided for under section 98 and schedule 2 to the act to comply with the regulator’s
objective under section 3 of the act and under regulations 32 to 34 of the Buildings (Higher-Risk
Buildings Procedures) (England) Regulations 2023).
Trigger All events meeting the mandatory occurrence reporting (MOR) criteria, and/or
reported through complaints (within the scope of BSR) or other intelligence
received or gathered by BSR.
Amount £151 per hour worked for BSR staff.
