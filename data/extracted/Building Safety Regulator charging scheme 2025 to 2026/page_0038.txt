Description
Inspections (audits) by BSR of building control bodies (local authorities or RBCAs). (Inspection
powers under section 58Z8 of the Building Act 1984).
Trigger Inspection (audit) by BSR on a periodic or proactive basis.
Amount payable £130 per hour worked for BSR staff.
Any costs to BSR of relevant authorities and third parties.
Payable by The building control body (local authority or RBCA).
