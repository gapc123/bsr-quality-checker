The determination of an application for a building assessment certificate under section 81 of the
act.
Description
Assessment of an application (including any accompanying documents) for a building
assessment certificate for an in scope building, including the costs to BSRof carrying out any
inspections required in connection with the application, and of establishing whether or not to
grant the certificate.
(As provided for under sections 79 to 81, and any regulations made under sections 80 or 81 of
the act).
Trigger Submission of an application for a building assessment certificate.
Amount £302 charge with application.
payable
