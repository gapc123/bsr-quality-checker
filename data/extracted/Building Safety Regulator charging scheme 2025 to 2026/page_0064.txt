Where charges are applied and paid but subsequently determined repayable by BSR(for
example, if they have been paid in error, if an appeal against the action taken by the regulator is
upheld, or following query or dispute) the repayment must be made by BSR within 30 days of
the repayment decision.
Annex D: queries and disputes process
The following outlines the procedure for answering queries and resolving disputes regarding
charges made by BSR.
This procedure aims to ensure that queries and disputes are resolved promptly, transparently
and fairly.
Level 1: routine enquiries
Queries from regulated parties about information contained in the request for payment which
could include:
● composition of charges
● amount of the request (not the rates as included in the charging scheme)
● method of payment
