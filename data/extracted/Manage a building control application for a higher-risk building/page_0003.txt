● PDFs

Charges
You’ll need to pay:
● £189 to apply for building control approval
● £189 to apply for change control
● £227 to apply for a full or partial completion certificate
You can pay this charge by debit card, credit card, or invoice.
You’ll also be charged:
● £151 per hour for Building Safety Regulator (BSR) staff to work on your case
● for any costs BSR incur from relevant authorities or third parties
There is no charge to:
● submit a notice of intent to start work or commencement
● tell us you have reached a certain inspection point
● apply for building control approval, if your building work is being transferred from another
building control body and that work has not sufficiently progressed with full plans
