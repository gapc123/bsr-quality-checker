You can use this service to apply for approval to construct, or carry out building work on, a
higher-risk building.
After you submit your application, you can return to this service to:
● make a major or notifiable change to the proposed work
● change the client, principal designer or principal contractor
● submit a notice of intention to start work, commencement or inspection
● apply for a completion certificate
A higher-risk building has at least:
● 7 storeys or is at least 18 metres high
● 2 residential units or is a hospital or a care home
A higher-risk building with at least 2 residential units must be registered with BSR before people live
there.
