# Attlee AI: AI-Powered Regulatory Compliance for Building Safety

## The Problem

The UK construction industry faces a regulatory transformation following the Building Safety Act 2022. For higher-risk buildings (18m+ or 7+ storeys with residential units), the new Gateway process introduces unprecedented documentation requirements:

**Current Challenges:**
- **High rejection rates** at Gateway 2 due to incomplete or inconsistent submissions
- **Costly delays** - BSR charges £151/hour for assessment time, with rejected applications requiring resubmission
- **Document complexity** - Submissions involve multiple technical documents (fire strategies, architectural drawings, MEP specs) that must maintain internal consistency
- **Knowledge gap** - New regulatory regime with limited precedent and evolving interpretation
- **Resource drain** - Technical teams spending weeks preparing submissions without confidence in quality

**The Stakes:**
- Average Gateway 2 assessment: 40-80 hours of BSR review time (£6,000-£12,000)
- Rejection and resubmission: Additional 4-8 week delays + repeat fees
- Project holding costs during delays: £50,000-£200,000+
- Compliance uncertainty affecting project financing and timelines

---

## The Solution: BSR Quality Checker

Attlee AI's platform performs pre-submission quality diagnostics for Gateway 2 applications, combining:

1. **AI-Powered Document Analysis** - Automated extraction of key building information across multiple PDFs
2. **Consistency Checking** - Cross-document validation of critical fields (height, storeys, evacuation strategy, fire ratings)
3. **Regulatory Knowledge Base** - 15+ indexed regulatory documents including BSR guidance, approved documents, and statutory instruments
4. **Intelligent Findings** - Severity-rated issues with specific citations and recommended actions
5. **Professional Reports** - Export-ready reports in PDF, Markdown, or JSON

**How It Works:**
1. Upload Gateway 2 submission pack (fire strategy, drawings, schedules, etc.)
2. AI extracts key fields and checks against regulatory requirements
3. System identifies inconsistencies, gaps, and quality issues
4. Receive detailed report with actionable findings
5. Fix issues before BSR submission
